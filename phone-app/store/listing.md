## Description  (max 4000)
```
DreamLayer is a memory layer for the real world. It quietly remembers what you'd otherwise forget — the promise you made, the person you just met, where you left the bike — and hands it back the instant you need it. Private by architecture, and yours to run.

WHAT IT REMEMBERS FOR YOU
• Promises & commitments — "send Marcus the lease by Friday," surfaced before it slips.
• People — names, how you know them, what you talked about, and who owes what.
• Places & things — where you parked, what you left behind, the café that's cash-only.
• Your morning brief — the day's few things that actually matter, in one glance.
• Messages, hands-free — read your texts and mail on your glasses and reply with a tap. Nothing sends without your approval.

PRIVATE BY ARCHITECTURE
Your memory lives on your own devices, not our servers. There's no account and no tracking. Cloud AI is off by default and strictly opt-in — only then does a request's text reach the provider you choose. Turn on Incognito and nothing leaves your local network at all. You can erase everything at any time.

HOW IT WORKS
The phone is your hub. Pair the Brilliant Labs Halo glasses to capture the world hands-free, and — optionally — connect an always-on Mac as your "Brain" for a bigger local model and answers drawn from your own files and mail. It's smart, and it stays on your side.

TRY IT WITH NO HARDWARE
No glasses yet? Turn on Demo Mode (right from the welcome tour, or in Settings) to explore the whole app with sample data — your memories, people, messages, and morning brief — so you can see exactly how it feels.

A NOTE ON HONESTY
DreamLayer ships first on the Brilliant Labs Halo, which is still ahead of us — the software is built and working today, and we say plainly where the hardware plugs in. Everything you see in the app is the real product.
```
